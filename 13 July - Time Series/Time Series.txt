AR Model :
--------
 Predicts future based on past time ( t= t-1,t-2,...)
 yt = b0+b1yt-1 +b2yt-2 + ......

It has 2 component : ACF & PACF
  ACF --> (Auto Corelation Function)
  PACF --> (Partial Auto Corelation Function)




In, Regression we have 2 variable -- X&Y (exp --salary)
time series we have 1 variable time , 
  Relative between Current time with prevous time is called "LAGS"



CORELATIO V/S LAGS
corelation works with 2 variable x&y ; whereas LAGS works with  1 variable i.e previous time 


ACF ---> Direct & indirect effect of values in previous time lags with the current time period.
PACF ---> Only direct effect of values in previous time lags.





MA Model :
----------

Predict the future time based on past error 
 AR Model -- past time (t= t-1, t-2,t-3)
 MA Model -- past error ( e = E-1 , E-2, E-3)



ARMA Model :  (AR + MA)
------------
 it IS THE COMBINATON OF AR & MA Model



ARIMA Model :  (AR & MA + Integration)
-----------

Stationary :- Constant Mean & Constant Variance
 if any dataset has constant mean & constant variance , then only apply "TIME SERIES MODEL"

NON STATIONARY :- 
 what if data is non stationary on that case what you (no constant mean , no constant variance)
then apply this FORMULA :- Y(t)-Y(t-1)  to make it Stationary.

How to check the graph is Stationary or not =>
 1. Visualization or Visual Inspection
 2. Global check v/s Local check

ARIMA (ARMA + Stationary) Constant Mean & Constant Variance

ARIMAX Model : (ARIMA + Xtrogeneous Variable)
-------------
If , there is any change in data Model , due to any external variable then it is called ARIMAX model.
eg - Change of Flight prices due to ( Thunder storm, heavy rain) then we use ARIMAX Model



SARIMAX Model : (SARIMA + Xtrogeneous Variable) Seasonal
----------------
If , there is any change in data Model i.e the data model will be of "Seasonal", due to any external variable then it is called SARIMAX model.
